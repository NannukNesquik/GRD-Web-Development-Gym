ABOUT US

Bertu’s Gym is one of Malta’s longest-standing fitness spaces, built on decades of discipline, community, and real training. Located in Għargħur, we’ve been helping people of all levels push their limits since 1982.

Bertu's Way

We focus on real training. No gimmicks or trends. Just effective workouts, proper technique, and a supportive environment for all fitness levels.

From general fitness to boxing and personal training, we offer structured sessions designed to build strength, endurance, and confidence.

Bertu’s Gym is known for its loyal members and welcoming atmosphere. Whether you are just starting or have been training for years, you will find your place here.
